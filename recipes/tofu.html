Oven Baked Tofu

Ingredients

  • Tofu
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Cornstarch (optional)

Steps

  1. Cube the tofu
  2. Press out the water from the tofu for 30 minutes
  3. Preheat oven to 425
  4. Line baking tray with aluminium foil and apply olive oil
  5. Once tofu has been pressed, coat in cornstarch and season with salt and pepper to taste
  6. Add tofu to baking tray and cook for 32 minutes, flipping over the tofu halfway
  7. Remove from oven, let cool and enjoy!
