Ingredients

  • Steak
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Butter
  • Garlic cloves

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425
  2. Season steak generously, let sit for 10-15 minutes
  3. Heat olive oil in pan on medium high, until oil starts to smoke
  4. Cook steak in the pan, searing for 2-2.5 minutes per side
  5. Upon completion, put garlic cloves in pan and immediately place steak in pan in oven for 8-12 minutes
  6. After time is up, check steak internal temperature to verify cooking
  7. Remove from oven, put butter in pan and baste steak
  8. Enjoy!
