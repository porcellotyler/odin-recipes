Ingredients

  • Salmon
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic cloves
  • Lemon

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400
  2. Season salmon to your liking
  3. Coat your cast iron pan with olive oil and put the salmon in it, skin side down
  4. Add garlic cloves to your pan
  5. Cook in the oven for 32 minutes
  6. Remove from over and let cool
  7. Enjoy!
